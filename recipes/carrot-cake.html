Carrot Cake

Description:

A simple, moist, yummy carrot cake with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • 4 eggs.
  • 1 ¼ cups of vegetable oil.
  • 2 cups of white sugar.
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract.
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour.
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda.
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  • ½ teaspoon of salt.
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon.
  • 3 cups grated of carrots.
  • 1 cup chopped of pecans.

Frosting:

  • ½ cup of butter, softened.
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese, softened.
  • 4 cups of confectioners' sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans.

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together eggs, oil, white sugar and 2 of teaspoons vanilla. Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Stir in carrots and fold in pecans. Pour the result into a prepared mold.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 40 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Let the mold cool for 10 minutes and then, turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
  4. To make the frosting, in a medium bowl, combine butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and 1 teaspoon of vanilla. Beat until the mixture is smooth and creamy and stir in chopped pecans. Frost the cooled cake and enjoy!
